About Me

Thinker, Designer, Doer

I am a Data Science and Economics student at UC Berkeley, interested in how technology can be shaped into products people actually use and trust. I enjoy breaking down complex ideas, thinking through user flows, and creating clear, intuitive experiences, especially at the intersection of product, design, and data.

Outside the finished project, I care about the process: asking better questions, testing assumptions, and learning enough from each version to make the next one stronger.
